Careers

Get paid to deliver!

Your vehicle, your time

Grab your car, bike, or scooter and be a delivery driver whenever you want—for an hour, a weekend, or throughout the week.

Get paid quickly

When you get paid to deliver using FoodVizn, your earnings are automatically transferred to your bank account every week.

Track your earnings

Before you accept any order, you'll be able to see how much you'll get paid to deliver it. In addition, customers have the option to tip in-app.
